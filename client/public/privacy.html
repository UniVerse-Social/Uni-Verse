1) Scope & Who We Are

We provide a community platform for Fullerton students and locals featuring DMs, clubs, events, marketplace, swipe/match, and live games.

We are not affiliated with CSUF and are not an emergency service. Call 911 in an emergency.

U.S. residents only. You affirm you reside in the United States and access the Services from the U.S.

2) Personal Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information collected automatically, and information from service providers. We aim to avoid collecting precise geolocation.

A. You Provide

  • Account: name/username, email, phone number (for SMS verification), password or auth tokens.
  • Profile: photo(s) for swipe/match; bio; interests; club memberships.
  • Content & Activity: posts, comments, messages (DMs), event and club listings, marketplace listings, game stats.
  • Purchases: when you buy coins; payment processing is handled by app stores or payment processors; we receive limited transactional metadata (e.g., token, amount, status), not your full card number.
  • Reports & Support: content you send when you report an issue or contact support.

B. Collected Automatically

  • Device & Log Data: device model, OS, app version, language, network, approximate location from IP, timestamps, referrers, crash logs.
  • Cookies/SDKs: for authentication, security, analytics, and ads (Google Ads).

C. From Service Providers/Partners

Analytics, anti-abuse, and ads partners may provide aggregated or pseudonymous signals (e.g., ad measurement). We do not buy data brokers’ dossiers.

3) Why We Use Your Information (Purposes)

  • Provide the Services: account creation, login, verification (email/SMS), swipe/match, clubs, events, marketplace, games.
  • Safety & Integrity: moderation, spam/abuse prevention, security, enforcing our Terms/Guidelines.
  • Personalization: feed ranking, content suggestions, limited ad personalization where allowed.
  • Measurement & Improvement: analytics, debugging, crash reporting, research (aggregated or de-identified).
  • Payments: to deliver coins and manage entitlements.
  • Legal: comply with law, enforce agreements, respond to legal requests.

4) How We Share Information

We share information with:

  • Service Providers/Contractors: hosting, analytics, security, customer support, payment processing (for coins), SMS/email verification.
  • Advertising Partners: to show and measure Google Ads and similar placements. Where this constitutes “sharing” under California law for cross-context behavioral advertising, you may opt out (see §9).
  • Other Users: content you post (e.g., profile photo in swipe, marketplace listings, club/event posts) is visible per your settings.
  • Legal/Compliance: to comply with law or prevent harm.
  • Business Changes: in the event of a reorganization or project transfer.

We do not sell personal information for money. We may “share” identifiers and usage data with advertising partners for ad personalization/measurement.

5) Your Controls

  • Profile & Content: edit or delete posts and your profile in settings.
  • Communications: control some emails/notifications in settings (service messages may still be sent).
  • Ads: use the “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” link to opt out of cross-context advertising; also use platform ad settings (Apple/Google).
  • Cookies/SDKs: manage via in-app settings and device controls.

6) Data Retention

We keep information as long as your account is active or as needed for the purposes in this Policy.

  • Account data: retained while active; delete within ~30–90 days after account deletion (subject to backups/legal).
  • DMs & posts: retained until you delete or your account is deleted; content shared with others may persist in their accounts.
  • Security & logs: ~24 months.
  • Backups: typically ≤30 days rolling.
  • Ad measurement/analytics: typically ≤13 months.

Actual periods may vary; we’ll update this Policy if they materially change.

7) Children & Students

The Services are for users 13+ only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we learn a child under 13 has used the Services, we will delete the account and associated information.

8) Security

We use organizational and technical measures such as HTTPS, access controls, encryption at rest (where supported), and monitoring. No system is 100% secure. Report issues to security@fullertonconnect.com.

9) California Privacy Rights (CPRA)

  • Right to Know/Access, Delete, and Correct. Request a copy of your data, deletion, or corrections.
  • Right to Opt-Out of “Sale”/“Sharing.” If we engage in cross-context ad “sharing,” you may opt out via our “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information” link. We also honor global privacy control (GPC) signals where feasible.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive PI. We use phone number only for verification and security; we do not use sensitive PI for additional purposes.
  • Non-Discrimination. We won’t discriminate for exercising your rights.

How to Exercise Rights

Use in-app controls or email privacy@fullertonconnect.com with “California Request” and verify your email/SMS code. Authorized agents must provide proof of authorization and we will verify the user.

Notice at Collection (Summary)

We collect: identifiers (email, phone, device IDs), internet activity (logs, cookies/SDKs), user content, and approximate location from IP. Purposes: provide & secure the Services, personalization, measurement, ads, and legal compliance. Sharing: service providers; ads/measurement partners (opt-out available). Retention: see §6.

10) Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies/SDKs for login, fraud prevention, analytics, and ads. You can manage these in settings or via your device/platform.

11) International Users

We are U.S.-only and host data in the United States. Do not use the Services from outside the U.S.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email. Continued use after the effective date means you accept the updated Policy.
