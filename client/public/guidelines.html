Golden Rules

  • Be respectful. No harassment, threats, hate, or targeted abuse.
  • Be authentic. No impersonation, fake engagement, or scams.
  • Be lawful. Follow laws and CSUF/city rules when applicable.
  • Be safe. No endangering others, doxxing, or non-consensual content.
  • Be relevant. Keep content local to the Fullerton community.

What’s Not Allowed

Safety & Harm

  • Violence or credible threats; incitement to harm.
  • Suicide or self-harm promotion (we may surface help lines).
  • Sexual exploitation or sexualized content involving minors (zero tolerance).
  • Dangerous activities encouraging injury or property damage.

Harassment & Hate

  • Bullying, stalking, dogpiling, or glorifying violence.
  • Slurs, stereotypes, or dehumanization targeting protected classes.
  • Content intended to shame private individuals (doxxing, revenge porn).

Privacy & Consent

  • Non-consensual intimate imagery; upskirting; hidden-camera content.
  • Sharing private info (address, phone, student IDs) without consent.

Illegal or Prohibited Transactions

Includes (not exhaustive):

  • Weapons, ammunition, explosives; drugs or paraphernalia;
  • Counterfeit or stolen goods; human/animal parts;
  • Adult sexual content/services; alcohol or tobacco;
  • Recalled/unsafe items; financial instruments or personal data;
  • Academic cheating materials; anything illegal or banned by campus housing policies.

Platform Integrity

  • Spam, phishing, malware, or coordinated inauthentic behavior.
  • Evasion of bans, selling accounts, or mass-creating throwaways.
  • Buying/selling coins outside approved channels.

Intellectual Property

  • Copyright/trademark infringement; pirated materials. Respect creators’ rights.

Misinformation in Emergencies

False claims that would reasonably cause harm (e.g., fake emergency alerts). We may label, reduce reach, or remove.

Feature-Specific Rules

Swipe/Match (Profile Photos)

  • Use your own recent photo or one you have rights to.
  • No explicit nudity or sexual content.
  • No minors-only photos that could be misused; do not upload others without consent.

DMs

  • No unsolicited sexual content, harassment, or threats.
  • Respect no-contact requests and blocks.

Clubs & Events

  • Be accurate about time/place; don’t imply CSUF sponsorship without written authorization.
  • Follow venue rules, permits, and noise/curfew laws.
  • No hazing or coercion.

Marketplace

  • Follow the Prohibited Items list.
  • Use in-app messaging; meet in public; keep receipts.
  • Listings must be local, truthful, and priced in USD.

Live Multiplayer Games

  • No cheating, exploits, or harassment.
  • We may reset scores, revoke perks, or suspend accounts for abuse.

Reporting & Moderation

  • Report content or users via in-app tools or abuse@fullertonconnect.com.
  • We use automated systems + human review.
  • Possible actions: label/blur, remove, limit features, warnings/strikes, suspensions, bans, device/number blocks.
  • You may appeal certain decisions in the app.

Age, Identity, and Verification

  • 13+ only. If you’re under 13, do not use the app.
  • We may verify via email and SMS codes. Don’t share verification codes.
  • Do not impersonate CSUF staff or other individuals.

Ads, Promotions, and Self-Promotion

  • Disclose paid partnerships and follow applicable laws and guidelines.
  • No deceptive or illegal promotions.
  • Ads may appear in sidebars and placements; we don’t endorse third-party products.

Safety Reminders

  • Meet in public, well-lit places; tell a friend; keep chats in-app.
  • Never share passwords or verification codes.
  • Call 911 in an emergency.

Consequences

  • Repeated or severe violations can lead to permanent ban.
  • We may notify law enforcement where required or appropriate.
  • Attempts to evade enforcement (new accounts, device spoofing) may result in broader blocks.

Final Notes

  • We may update these Guidelines; material changes will be announced in-app.
  • Not affiliated with CSUF. Use of the app does not create any relationship with the university.
