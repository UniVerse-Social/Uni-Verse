Golden Rules

  • Be respectful. No harassment, threats, hate, or targeted abuse.
  • Be authentic. No impersonation, fake engagement, or scams.
  • Be lawful. Follow applicable laws and campus rules where relevant.
  • Be safe. No endangering others, doxxing, or non-consensual content.
  • Be relevant. Keep content appropriate for a nationwide student community.

What’s Not Allowed

Safety & Harm

  • Violence or credible threats; incitement to harm.
  • Suicide or self-harm promotion (we may surface help lines or resources).
  • Sexual exploitation or sexualized content involving minors (zero tolerance).
  • Dangerous activities encouraging injury or property damage.

Harassment & Hate

  • Bullying, stalking, dogpiling, or glorifying violence.
  • Slurs, stereotypes, or dehumanization targeting protected classes.
  • Content intended to shame private individuals (including non-consensual intimate imagery).

Privacy & Consent

  • Non-consensual intimate imagery; upskirting; hidden-camera content.
  • Sharing private info (home address, phone, student IDs, personal email) without consent.
  • Posting images of others without permission in sensitive or embarrassing contexts.

Illegal or Prohibited Transactions

Includes (not exhaustive):

  • Weapons, ammunition, explosives; illegal drugs or paraphernalia.
  • Counterfeit or stolen goods; human/animal parts.
  • Adult sexual content/services; alcohol or tobacco where prohibited.
  • Recalled/unsafe items; financial instruments or personal data.
  • Academic cheating materials; anything illegal or banned by housing/campus policies.

Platform Integrity

  • Spam, phishing, malware, or coordinated inauthentic behavior.
  • Evasion of bans, selling accounts, or mass-creating throwaways.
  • Buying/selling UniVerse coins or items outside approved channels.

Intellectual Property

  • Copyright/trademark infringement; pirated materials. Respect creators’ rights.

Misinformation in Emergencies

False claims that would reasonably cause harm (e.g., fake emergency alerts, evacuation notices) may be labeled, downranked, or removed. We may act quickly where safety is involved.

Feature-Specific Rules

Profiles & Matching

  • Use your own recent photo or one you have rights to.
  • No explicit nudity or sexual content.
  • No minors-only photos meant for adults; do not upload others without consent.

DMs

  • No unsolicited sexual content, harassment, or threats.
  • Respect no-contact requests and blocks.

Clubs & Events

  • Be accurate about time/place; don’t imply university sponsorship without written authorization.
  • Follow venue rules, permits, and local laws.
  • No hazing or coercion.

Marketplace

  • Follow the Prohibited Items list.
  • Use in-app messaging; meet in public; keep receipts.
  • Listings must be truthful, local to your area, and priced in USD.

Live Multiplayer Games

  • No cheating, exploits, or harassment.
  • We may reset scores, revoke perks, or suspend accounts for abuse.

Reporting & Moderation

  • Report content or users via in-app tools or at abuse@uni-verse.social.
  • We use a mix of automated systems and human review.
  • Actions can include: labeling/blur, removal, feature limits, warnings/strikes, suspensions, bans, or device/number blocks.
  • You may be able to appeal certain decisions in the app.

Age, Identity, and Verification

  • 13+ only. If you’re under 13, do not use UniVerse.
  • We may verify that you’re a student or eligible user using your email (for example, certain school domains) and SMS codes. Using a student email does not mean your school operates or endorses UniVerse.
  • Do not impersonate university staff, administrators, or other individuals.

Ads, Promotions, and Self-Promotion

  • Disclose paid partnerships and follow applicable laws and guidelines.
  • No deceptive, misleading, or illegal promotions.
  • Ads that appear in UniVerse do not mean we endorse third-party products.

Safety Reminders

  • Meet in public, well-lit places; tell a friend where you’re going.
  • Keep chats in-app until you trust someone.
  • Never share passwords or verification codes with anyone.
  • Call 911 or your local emergency number in an emergency.

Consequences

  • Repeated or severe violations can lead to a permanent ban.
  • We may notify law enforcement where required or appropriate.
  • Attempts to evade enforcement (new accounts, device spoofing) may result in broader blocks.

Final Notes

  • We may update these Guidelines; material changes may be announced in-app.
  • UniVerse is an independent project and is not affiliated with any college or university.

These Community Guidelines are incorporated by reference into the Terms of Service. See also our Privacy Policy.
